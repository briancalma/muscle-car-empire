2017 Chevrolet Camaro ZL1

The Real American Muscle

About the Car

So far, the Z28 is taking a breather from the Chevrolet Camaro lineup. That leaves the new ZL1 is a 650 horsepower monster equipped with a 6.2-liter supercharged LT4V8 engine that knocks out 650 pound-feet of tourque. Top speed is 195 miles per hour in just 3.5 seconds, running the quarter mile in under 12 seconds
This easily recognizable sport coupe was redesigned last year. It boasts a more aerodynamic body, a completely redesigned dashboard and three new engines ranging from the 275-horsepower, 2.0L four cylinder to the 6.2L V8 in the SS producing 455 horsepower. GM is going a step further this year: in addition to the 50th anniversary versions, there's the ZL1 with its 6.2L supercharged V8 that generates 650 horsepower.
